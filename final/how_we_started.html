How We Started

We started in the summer of 2014. The main driving force for this change was I lost my job and have no way to do it. I used to install satellites but the company went under so I looked for something to do and a good friend from church offered that we start ranching. So that is exactly what we did. It was a massive change of life. I am thankful that my friend helped us along the path and now I can't imagine living any other way.

Starting Ranch

Our ranch started out as only 100 areas and 5 horses all quarter horses. From there we went to the auction where with the help of our dear friends we bought 50 head. That was everything we had. We now have 100-125 head a year that we raise a year. We also now have 200 acres.
